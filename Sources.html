Our Goal

The project is dedicated to revolutionizing the efficiency and performance of VTOL UAVs by integrating avian-inspired design, flexible wing structures using Shape Memory Alloys, and adaptive machine learning interfaces.

The ultimate goal is to significantly enhance flight range, control, and efficiency, thereby transforming the economic and social landscape of UAV applicability.
