History

770 BC and 221 BC: Chinese waring states period- Muyuan kites.

The inception of flight mechanisms can be traced back to this period. Infantrymen flew kites, known as Muyuan, constructed from lightweight wooden frames. The design and orientation of these kites were entirely inspired by avian species. This marked the commencement of the journey towards flight, driven by detailed observations and studies of birds by scholars and travelers of the time.

History

1915, United States:

The National Advisory Committee for Aeronautics (NACA) was established, marking a significant step in the evolution of flight mechanisms. NACA aimed to harness the potential of flight for research towards modern airplanes. As the organization expanded, it developed a systematic database of airfoils using their 4-digit and 6-digit classification systems.

Modern:

Over the past decade, researchers have started exploring biomimetic airfoils, trying to incorporate features from bird wings. However, implementing these models is challenging due to the need for system overhauls and custom infrastructure, which increase costs without improving efficiency.

Our Goal
